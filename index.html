Support Article Formatter

Convert support articles to accordion format for CRM platforms

How to Use This Tool:

  1. Enter your Anthropic API key (get one at console.anthropic.com)
  2. Paste your HTML article content in the input area
  3. Click "Format Article" to convert to accordion format
  4. Copy the formatted result and paste into your CRM system

Security Note: Your API key is only used for this session and is not stored or transmitted elsewhere. It's sent directly to Anthropic's servers for processing.
